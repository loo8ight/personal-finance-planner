# Insurance And Protection

Use this for protection-gap checks. Do not tell the user to buy, cancel, or switch a specific policy definitively.

## Core Protection Layers

- Emergency fund
- Health insurance and National Health Insurance context
- Indemnity medical insurance
- Critical illness/cancer coverage
- Life insurance if dependents rely on income
- Disability or income-protection planning
- Driver insurance if relevant

## Insurance Premium Burden

Compare monthly premiums with income and essential spending. High premium burden can weaken cash flow, but cancellation can create coverage gaps and re-entry problems.

## Dependents

If the user has children, spouse, parents, or other dependents, review:

- Income replacement need
- Medical-cost risk
- Caregiving obligations
- Emergency fund target

## Review Checklist

- Policy purpose
- Insured amount
- Premium
- Renewal/non-renewal
- Exclusions
- Duplicated coverage
- Waiting periods
- Surrender value
- Health underwriting implications

## Caution

Before cancelling or replacing insurance, the user should review policy documents and consult a qualified insurance professional if the decision is material.
