# Korea Accounts

Use this reference to map account types to planning purposes.

## Deposit

Purpose: preserve principal for short-term goals or emergency cash.  
Check: maturity, early withdrawal, rate changes, deposit protection status.

## Installment Savings

Purpose: disciplined accumulation for known goals.  
Check: monthly contribution, maturity, early cancellation rules.

## CMA

Purpose: brokerage cash management and idle cash.  
Check: product type, risk, protection, withdrawal access.

## Parking Account

Purpose: flexible reserve cash.  
Check: rate tiers, withdrawal limits, institution risk, protection.

## ISA

Purpose: tax-aware medium-term savings/investment wrapper.  
Check: eligibility, contribution limit, tax treatment, lock-up period, current law.

## Pension Savings

Purpose: long-term retirement saving.  
Check: tax credit, investment options, withdrawal restrictions, pension receipt rules.

## IRP

Purpose: retirement saving and retirement-pay handling.  
Check: tax credit, withdrawal restrictions, fees, investment limits, receipt rules.

## Workplace Retirement Pension

Types:

- DB: benefit is generally formula-based.
- DC: user/employer contribution account; investment choice can matter.
- IRP: individual account for retirement assets.

Check plan documents and employer rules.

## Housing Subscription Savings

Purpose: cheongyak eligibility and housing planning.  
Check subscription period, region, household status, deposits, points, and current rules.

## Matching Accounts To Goals

- Daily spending: demand deposit.
- Emergency fund: parking account, demand deposit, short-term deposit.
- 1-3 year goal: cash-like products and low-volatility choices.
- Long-term retirement: pension savings, IRP, workplace pension, diversified long-term assets.
- Housing: housing subscription savings plus separate cash reserve.
