# Life Events

Use this to update plans when life changes.

## Moving Out

Review deposit, monthly housing cost, furniture/appliances, emergency fund, commute, and contract risk.

## Job Change

Review income stability, probation period, severance/retirement pension, insurance, commute, and tax documents.

## Resignation

Review runway, health insurance, National Pension status, severance, debt payments, job-search timeline, and fallback plan.

## Freelance Transition

Review variable income reserve, client pipeline, tax filing, health insurance, National Pension, expense tracking, and contracts.

## Business Launch

Review launch runway, fixed costs, VAT/income tax, business registration, insurance, debt risk, and separate business accounts.

## Marriage

Review household budget, debt disclosure, housing, insurance, emergency fund, family support, and goals.

## Childbirth

Review income interruption, childcare, housing, insurance, emergency fund, parental leave, and public benefits requiring current confirmation.

## Housing Purchase

Review down payment, mortgage stress, taxes/fees, maintenance, liquidity, job stability, and long-term family plan.

## Jeonse Move

Review deposit safety, guarantee insurance, loan burden, renewal risk, and liquidity.

## Parent Support

Review recurring support, medical risk, siblings/family agreements, emergency fund, and insurance.

## Pet Spending

Review recurring food/care cost, medical reserve, insurance if applicable, and housing constraints.

## Inheritance/Gift

Treat inheritance and gift tax as professional-advice areas. Ask for broad context only and recommend legal/tax review.

## FIRE Or Early Retirement

Review healthcare, housing, long-term inflation, sequence risk, withdrawal assumptions, and re-entry options.
