# Interview Guide

Use this guide when creating or updating a user's financial profile.

## Operating Rules

- Ask 2 to 4 questions at a time.
- Summarize after each round.
- Label missing data as unknown instead of inventing it.
- Store only planning data, not direct identifiers.
- Use monthly KRW amounts unless the user provides a different basis.
- Separate confirmed facts from assumptions.

## Round 1. Basic Situation

Ask:

1. 나이대 또는 만 나이
2. 직업/소득 형태: 직장인, 프리랜서, 개인사업자, 무직, 학생 등
3. 가족상황: 1인 가구, 배우자, 자녀, 부모 부양 등
4. 거주지역과 주거형태: 자가, 전세, 월세, 가족 거주 등

Summarize household size, income stability, housing context, and obvious life-stage constraints.

## Round 2. Income

Ask:

1. 월 실수령액 또는 월평균 소득
2. 부수입, 사업소득, 프리랜서 소득
3. 변동소득의 월별 범위
4. 상여금/성과급/계절성 여부

Summarize stable income and variable income separately.

## Round 3. Spending

Ask:

1. 주거비, 통신비, 보험료, 대출상환 등 고정비
2. 식비, 교통비, 쇼핑, 취미 등 변동비
3. 구독비와 자동결제
4. 카드값과 할부

Summarize fixed-cost pressure, controllable categories, and unknown spending.

## Round 4. Debt

Ask for each debt:

- Type
- Balance
- Rate
- Minimum payment
- Maturity or remaining term

Flag revolving credit, card loans, delinquency risk, and high-rate unsecured debt for urgent review.

## Round 5. Assets

Ask for:

- Cash and cash-like assets
- Deposits and savings
- Investments
- Retirement accounts
- Real estate or jeonse deposit
- Other major assets

Summarize liquidity, concentration, and risk-asset exposure.

## Round 6. Accounts, Pension, Insurance

Ask about:

- ISA
- Pension savings
- IRP
- Workplace retirement pension
- Expected National Pension if known
- Indemnity medical insurance
- Life, cancer, income protection, driver insurance

Summarize protection gaps and account-usage opportunities without recommending specific products.

## Round 7. Goals

Ask:

- Emergency fund target
- Debt payoff goal
- Moving out, marriage, children, housing, job change, resignation, business launch
- Retirement or FIRE goal
- Time horizon and priority

Convert goals into near-term, 90-day, 1-year, and long-term planning buckets.
