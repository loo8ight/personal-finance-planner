# `/finance networth`

Use for calculating and organizing assets, liabilities, and net worth.

## Inputs

- Cash and cash-like assets
- Deposits and savings
- Investments
- Retirement accounts
- Real estate
- Jeonse deposit
- Other assets
- All debts

## Analyze

- Total assets
- Total liabilities
- Net worth
- Liquid asset ratio
- Risk asset ratio
- Real estate, cash, and investment concentration

Use `scripts/networth_calculator.py` when numeric values are available.

## Output

- Asset/liability table.
- Net worth summary.
- Concentration risks and liquidity notes.
- Missing or stale valuation list.

For housing or private business valuations, label values as estimates unless the user provides verified appraisals or statements.
