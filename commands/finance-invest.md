# `/finance invest`

Use for investment allocation review.

## Inputs

- Cash
- Deposits
- Korean stocks
- Overseas stocks
- ETFs/funds
- Pension accounts
- Bonds or bond-like assets
- Crypto or high-volatility assets
- Goal horizon and risk tolerance

## Analyze

- Asset allocation by category.
- Risk concentration.
- Time-horizon match.
- Rebalancing need.
- Whether cash reserve is adequate before taking investment risk.

Use `scripts/networth_calculator.py` for allocation percentages if helpful.

## Output

- Allocation table.
- Risk notes.
- Rebalancing checklist.
- Goal-horizon suggestions at the category level.

Never provide buy/sell instructions for individual securities, ETFs, funds, or cryptoassets. State that past returns do not guarantee future returns.
