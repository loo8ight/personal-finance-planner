# `/finance tax`

Use for tax-aware checklists for employees, freelancers, sole proprietors, investors, and retirement-account users.

## Inputs

- Income type: employee, freelancer, sole proprietor, mixed income, investor, retiree
- Approximate annual income by type
- Retirement-account contributions
- Medical, education, insurance, housing, donation, and card-spending items if relevant
- Overseas stock sales or financial income if relevant
- Business registration and VAT status if relevant

## Analyze

- Employee year-end settlement checklist.
- Freelancer 3.3 percent withholding and comprehensive income tax checklist.
- Sole proprietor expense, VAT, and income tax checklist.
- Financial income comprehensive taxation risk to verify.
- Overseas stock capital gains tax basic check.
- Pension savings and IRP tax-credit check.

## Output

- Tax checklist by income type.
- Missing documents list.
- Items to verify with official sources or a tax professional.
- Calendar reminders for next filing or review period.

Do not calculate or guarantee final tax owed, refund amount, deduction eligibility, or filing result.
