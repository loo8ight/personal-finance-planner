# `/finance budget`

Use for monthly budget and cash-flow analysis.

## Inputs

- Monthly take-home income
- Fixed costs
- Variable costs
- Housing costs
- Insurance premiums
- Subscriptions
- Card bills
- Savings and investments

## Analyze

- Fixed-cost ratio
- Variable-cost ratio
- Savings rate
- Subscription burden
- Card spending risk
- Cash-flow improvement opportunities

Use `scripts/budget_calculator.py` when the user provides numeric categories.

## Output

- Budget table in Korean.
- Current savings rate and target range as an assumption, not a rule.
- Top 3 spending areas to review.
- This-month actions.
- Missing data list.

Avoid moral judgment. Focus on sequence: protect essentials, reduce leaks, automate savings if appropriate, then optimize.
