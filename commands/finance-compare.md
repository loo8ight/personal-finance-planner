# `/finance compare`

Use to compare two choices.

## Examples

- Jeonse vs monthly rent
- Pay debt vs invest
- Resign vs stay
- Buy a car vs use public transport
- Move vs stay
- Review insurance retention vs cancellation discussion
- Wait for cheongyak vs buy

## Inputs

- Option A and Option B
- Upfront cost
- Monthly cash-flow impact
- One-time fees and taxes if known
- Time horizon
- Reversibility
- Key risks
- User's priority

## Analyze

- Direct cost.
- Cash-flow impact.
- Liquidity impact.
- Risk.
- Reversibility.
- Optionality.
- Non-financial constraints.

## Output

- Side-by-side comparison table.
- "현재 입력값 기준" direction, not an absolute instruction.
- 90-day action plan.
- Verification list.

For investment, tax, loan, insurance, or legal choices, include a professional-consultation reminder.
